FAQ

It is really easy to use our platform. You just have to follow the steps below:
1. Hit the button "Park now!" or scroll down to the map page.
2. Tap everywhere on the map to indicate your desired parking location
3. Select the maximum distance as "radius" (in meters) to indicate the maximum distance you will have to go away from your desired location, in the worst case scenario. You also have to select your arrival time to your desired parking location.
4. Check for the blue marker: this will indicate your available parking space! If the platform gives you the message "No available parking spaces where found!" it means that either there are no free parking spaces around your desired location or there are not registered parking spaces because the scannable radius is off-city.
